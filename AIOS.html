Gemini OS - Concept

This is an interactive mockup of a desktop OS with a "metal bone" aesthetic.
- Windows are draggable.
- The central window contains an interactive 3D "artifact". You can rotate it with your mouse.
- The database window showcases your idea of an exportable list of materials.
- The taskbar at the bottom includes a clock and app icons.
